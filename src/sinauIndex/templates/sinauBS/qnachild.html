Is Mayonnaise Instrument?

All; Patrick Star


QnA Math Physics English Biology Literature Ask Question

Yes Patrick,
An instrument can be defined as a tool or implement. It is possible to use mayonnaise as a tool as it can be used to bind loose items to bread in a sandwich, e.g. by spreading mayo on the bread first, Olive salad (or muffaletta) sticks to the bread better making your sandwich less sloppy. So in the strictest sense you could call mayo an instrument, but most English speakers would not use the word that way.
